ABOUT THE PLATFORM

Structured data, clearly labelled.

BetIntel is a prototype monitoring platform designed to organize publicly visible operator information into a fast and transparent dashboard.

What the platform does

It records scan times, extraction status and public text in a consistent data format that can be searched and compared.

What it does not do

It does not guarantee accuracy, availability, eligibility, outcomes or financial returns. Automated text is not treated as verified without manual review.
