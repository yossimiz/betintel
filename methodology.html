TRANSPARENT PROCESS

Methodology

1. Collection

A scheduled Node.js process requests configured public pages and converts visible HTML into plain text. It searches for limited offer-related patterns and records a timestamp.

2. Status labels

Detected means matching text was found automatically. It does not mean the offer was independently verified. Needs review means the page loaded without reliable extraction. Unavailable means the request failed, timed out or was blocked.

3. Integrity

The system does not replace failed results with invented ratings, payment speeds, RTP values, reviews or promotional claims.

4. Limitations

Public pages can differ by country, language, account status, age and eligibility. Automated extraction may be incomplete or inaccurate and should be checked manually.
